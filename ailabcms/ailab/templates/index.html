Welcome to Ailab Research Group

Aliab Research Group belongs to the College of Computing and Software Engineering at Kennesaw State University led by Dr. Maria Valero. The group is focused on applications, technologies, and services in IoT for security and healthcare, cyber-physical systems, services for decentralized computing, services for data-sharing, and sensor networks. Our projects cover a broad spectrum of IoT and service applications such as non-invasive sensors for vital signs estimation, sensors for security applications, microservices for portability of applications, models for decentralized and in-situ computing, privacy applications on sensor networks, and more.

Research

IoT as a service, IoT for security and healthcare, microservices for sensor decentralized computing and sensor networks. Security and privacy.
